## 描述

该工作流程实现以下工作：  
- 允许您在以下层级设定目标：    
	- 每年  
	- 每月  
	- 每周
	- 每日

## 使用到的插件

- Obsidian Templater
- Obsidian Buttons
- Obsidian QuickAdd

## Instructions

1. Fill out [[My Core Principles]] with the principles you hold dearest.
2. From the Command Palette (`CMD/CTRL P`), select `Periodic Notes: Open yearly note`.
3. In the yearly note ([[Yearly Review 2022]]), put your cursor on the line you want the new OKR (objective) to be in, and then hit `CMD '`. Select `Add Yearly OKR`. Type out your new objective, and hit ENTER. The new page will be created.
4. When prompted, enter a name for your objective (you can change this later). The new page is created.
5. Fill out the sections of your new objective.
6. Do steps 3-5 until you've made enough yearly objectives.
7. Open up the Command Palette again and select `Periodic Notes: Open quarterly note`. A new quarterly note is created.
8. Open up the Command Palette again and select `Periodic Notes: Open monthly note`. A new monthly note that includes your OKRs is created. The `Last Month` section is populated with the previous month's initiatives, if there is one.
9. Fill out the sections in the monthly note.
10. Open up the Command Palette again and this time, select `Periodic Notes: Open weekly note`. This creates a new weekly note that has a `Monthly initiatives` section that contains what you filled in for the monthly review. If you have a previous weekly note, it is also populated for you on this note.
说明

11. 在命令面板（“CMD/CTRL P”）中选择“周期性笔记：打开年度笔记”。
12. 在年度笔记（[[2026]]]）中，将光标放在你希望新目标（OKR）所在的行上，然后点击“CMD”。选择“添加年度OKR”。输入你的新目标，然后按回车。新页面将被创建。
13. 提示时输入目标名称（之后可更改）。新页面被创建。
14. 填写你新目标的各个部分。
15. 完成第3-5步，直到你设定了足够的年度目标。
16. 再次打开命令调色板，选择“周期性笔记：打开月度笔记”。会创建一个包含你OKR的新月度记录。“上个月”部分会显示上个月的倡议（如果有的话）。
17. 填写月度笔记中的各部分。
18. 再次打开命令调色板，这次选择“周期性笔记：打开每周笔记”。这会创建一个新的每周笔记，其中包含“每月计划”部分，记录你为月度回顾填写的内容。如果你之前有每周笔记，也会在这条笔记中自动填充。

	From now on, you can use these commands to create new yearly, quarterly, monthly, and weekly notes:
- `Periodic Notes: Open yearly note
- `Periodic Notes: Open monthly note`
- `Periodic Notes: Open weekly note`
-  `Periodic Notes: Open daily note`
